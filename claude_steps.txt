# Session: March 11, 2026 - Events/Places Enhancements and Sharing

## Summary of Changes
- **Events & Places Editing:**
  - Added `PUT /events/:id` and `PUT /places/:id` backend routes and controllers.
  - Enabled creator-only (and admin) editing functionality in `EventsPage.tsx` and `PlacesTab.tsx` with a unified Save/Update dialog.
- **Event Tagging:**
  - Added a `type` field to the `events` table (matching `places` tags: Work, Study, Fitness, Social, Performance).
  - Updated the Event creation/edit dialog to include this tag selector.
- **Verified Attendee Lists & Rewards:**
  - Implemented `event_checkins` table to record verified QR scans.
  - **Rewards:** Both the attendee and the organizer now receive **10 PraxisPoints** upon a successful QR check-in.
  - Creators can now view a list of verified attendees for their past events via a new "People" icon on the event card.
- **Enhanced Sharing (References):**
  - Updated `ReferenceCard` and `ReferencePicker` to support **Places** as shareable attachments in posts and messages.
  - Unified the attachment logic across `ChatRoom`, `PostFeed`, and `GroupRoom`.
