## Strategic Pivot (March 2026)
Praxis = daily goal journal + accountability buddy app.

Goal tree = emotional living journal.
Daily loop = morning brief, one-tap check-in, evening recap.
Matching = sparring partner requests.
Social proof = leaderboard, shareable snippets, embeddable streak widget.

Key architectural changes:
- **Matching**: rule-based (domain overlap + progress similarity), no LLM
- **AI coaching**: template-based by default, real LLM only on premium "Axiom Boost"
- **Grading/streaks/analytics**: pure local math, no AI
- **Betting/streaks/leaderboard/viz**: 100% AI-free
