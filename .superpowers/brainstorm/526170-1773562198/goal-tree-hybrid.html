Hybrid: Collapsible Tree + Drill-Down Workspace

The tree stays visible for navigation. Tapping a goal opens its workspace inline or as an expanding panel. Which expansion style?

A

Inline Expand Recommended

Tapping a goal expands it in-place, pushing siblings down. The workspace (tracker, sub-goals, actions) appears right under the node. Tap again or another node to collapse.

Bottom Sheet Workspace
